Most learning platforms have the same shape. There is a library of video courses. You watch them. You maybe pass a quiz at the end. Then you leave and try to remember what you watched a week later.

That model is not bad, but it is missing something: the person who helps you understand, not just consume.

[Mentorize](https://mentorize.me) is the platform I built to fix that. It started as a solo project and grew into a multi-product learning system, built under the tagline: *"Where mentors help you memorize."*

## What the platform actually is

Mentorize is a suite of tools that work together, not one isolated app:

**The learning app** at [app.mentorize.me](https://app.mentorize.me) is where courses, quizzes, and flashcards live. You can create your own learning modules, share them with others, and access a growing library of community content. Content can be public, private, or shared with a specific team.

**The AI mentor** at [ai.mentorize.me](https://ai.mentorize.me) is a chat interface connected to all major AI providers and their models. It is built specifically for learning: it can quiz you, explain concepts in different ways, recommend courses based on what you are studying, generate new practice material on the fly, and respond in over 80 languages.

**The team chat** at [chat.mentorize.me](https://chat.mentorize.me) is real-time messaging with channels, direct messages, and voice and video. Learning is social. This is where study groups and teams work together.

**The cloud drive** at [drive.mentorize.me](https://drive.mentorize.me) is where media, documents, and course materials live. Upload once, reference from anywhere on the platform.

## Why it is different

Three things I kept coming back to while building it.

First, the AI should teach, not just answer. The AI mentor is context-aware. It knows what you are learning, can pull from your course materials, and adapts its explanations when something is not landing. That is different from pasting a question into a general chatbot.

Second, privacy is not negotiable. Zero data selling. The platform is GDPR compliant and designed from day one with user data protection as a requirement, not an afterthought.

Third, it should be free to start. The HOBBY tier is free forever: core learning features, limited AI usage, no credit card required. You can do a lot before you need to upgrade.
